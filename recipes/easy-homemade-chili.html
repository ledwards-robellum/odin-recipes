Easy Homemade Chili

Back to Home

Description

This easy homemade chili recipe is a hearty and flavorful dish that's perfect for a cozy meal. Packed with ground beef, beans, and tomatoes, it's a comforting classic that can be enjoyed on its own or with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 (15 oz) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (15 oz) can kidney beans
  • 1 (14.5 oz) can stewed tomatoes
  • 1 1/2 cups water (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Combine ground beef and onion in a large saucepan over medium heat; stir until meat is brown and onion is tender, 5 to 7 minutes.
  2. Stir in tomato sauce, kidney beans, and stewed tomatoes. For a thinner consistency, add water. Add chili powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, cover and let simmer, about 15 minutes.
